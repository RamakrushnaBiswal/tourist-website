Puri

Puri, a coastal town in Odisha, India, is renowned for its iconic Jagannath Temple, a Hindu pilgrimage site, and its annual Rath Yatra festival. Visitors can relax on the beautiful Puri Beach, explore the architectural marvel of the Konark Sun Temple nearby, and witness the vibrant culture at events like the Puri Sea Beach Festival and Puri Beach Festival. Chilika Lake, Asia's largest coastal lagoon, is a short drive away, offering birdwatching and boat rides. Don't miss the opportunity to savor delicious Odia cuisine,

Similipal

Similipal National Park is a tiger reserve and wildlife sanctuary in the Mayurbhanj district of Odisha, India. The park is named after the red silk cotton trees that grow in the area.The park is part of the Mayurbhanj Elephant Reserve, which includes three protected areas. It has been part of the UNESCO World Network of Biosphere Reserves since 2009.
